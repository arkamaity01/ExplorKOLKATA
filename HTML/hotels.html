3Night/4Day package

Day 1:
Explore Historical and Cultural Sites
Morning: Arrival: Arrive in Kolkata and check into your hotel.
Breakfast: Enjoy a traditional Bengali breakfast at a local restaurant.Check into hotel
Afternoon: Victoria Memorial:Visit the iconic Victoria Memorial, a stunning white marble structure surrounded by beautiful gardens.St. Paul's Cathedral:Explore St. Paul's Cathedral, a masterpiece of Indo-Gothic architecture.
Evening: Maidan and Princep Ghat:Take a leisurely stroll in Maidan, a vast open field, and visit Princep Ghat along the Hooghly River.
Dinner: Have dinner at a local restaurant or try street food.

Day 2:
Cultural Exploration
Morning:Indian Museum:Spend the morning at the Indian Museum, one of the oldest and largest museums in India.
Afternoon:Dakshineswar Kali Temple and Belur Math:Visit Dakshineswar Kali Temple and Belur Math, two significant religious landmarks on the banks of the Hooghly River.
Evening:Howrah Bridge:Take a boat ride on the Hooghly River for a unique view of the Howrah Bridge. Shopping at New Market:Explore the bustling New Market for shopping and souvenirs.
Dinner:Have dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 3:
Art and Culture
Morning: Mother House:Visit Mother Teresa's Missionaries of Charity at Mother House.
Afternoon: Kala Bhavan and Academy of Fine Arts:Explore the vibrant arts scene in Kolkata by visiting Kala Bhavan and the Academy of Fine Arts.
Evening:Park Street:Enjoy the lively atmosphere of Park Street, known for its restaurants, cafes, and nightlife.
Dinner:Have dinner at one of the popular restaurants on Park Street.

Day 4:
Heritage and Departure
Morning:Kumartuli:Visit Kumartuli, the potters' quarter, known for its clay idols and traditional craftsmanship.
Afternoon:Lunch and Free Time: Have lunch and spend your afternoon shopping for last-minute souvenirs or relaxing at your hotel.
Evening:Departure:Depending on your departure time, you may have some free time for additional exploration or relaxation.Airport Transfer:Head to the airport for your departure.

4Night/5Day package

Day 1:
Arrival in Kolkata
Morning: Arrival and Check-In Arrive in Kolkata and check into your chosen accommodation.Check into hotel
Afternoon: Explore South KolkataVisit the iconic Victoria Memorial. Stroll through the beautiful Maidan
Evening: Park StreetHead to Park Street for dinner at one of the many restaurants. Experience the vibrant nightlife of the city.

Day 2:
Historical Kolkata
Morning: Howrah Bridge and Flower Market Start your day at the iconic Howrah Bridge. Visit the bustling Mullick Ghat Flower Market.
Afternoon:Indian Museum and Marble Palace Explore the Indian Museum, one of the oldest museums in Asia. Visit Marble Palace, known for its stunning architecture and art collection.
Evening: Prinsep Ghat Enjoy a relaxing evening at Prinsep Ghat along the Hooghly River. Take a boat ride if available.

Day 3:
Cultural Immersion
Morning: Mother House and Kalighat Temple Visit Mother Teresa's Missionaries of Charity at Mother House. Explore the Kalighat Temple, one of Kolkata's holiest sites.
Afternoon: College Street and Coffee House Stroll through College Street, known for its bookstores. Relax at the iconic Coffee House, a hub for intellectuals and artists.
Evening:Rabindra Sadan Attend a cultural performance at Rabindra Sadan.

Day 4:
Art and Literature
Morning: Jorasanko Thakur Bari Explore the ancestral home of the Tagore family.Visit the Tagore Museum.
Afternoon: Park Street Cultural Hub Visit the Academy of Fine Arts. Explore the various art galleries on Park Street.
Evening: Street Food at Dacre Street Enjoy Kolkata's famous street food at Dacre Street.

Day 5:
Departure
Morning: Dakshineswar Kali Temple Visit the Dakshineswar Kali Temple along the banks of the Hooghly River.
Afternoon: Shopping at New MarketShop for souvenirs and local crafts at New Market.
Evening: Departure Depending on your departure time, you might have some time for last-minute exploration or relaxation.
